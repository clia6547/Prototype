Privacy Policy for NeighbourLink

Welcome to NeighbourLink! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data and information. This Privacy Policy reflects how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you avail our ride-sharing services.

1. Information Collection

We collect many types of information to provide and improve our services.

1.1 Private Information

  • Call, email, and postal addresses are included in the contact details.
  • The username, password, profile picture, and user preferences are all part of the account information.
  • Payment Data: Credit card details, billing address, and transaction history.

1.2 Location Information

  • Real-Time Positioning Data: GPS data and travel routes to improve navigation and provide ride services.
  • Location History: An itinerary of your past excursions and points of interest.

1.3 Usage Specifics

  • Service Utilization: Details regarding your dealings with our services, including bookings, quotes for transportation, and reviews.
  • Device information includes the IP address, operating system, kind of browser, and device identifiers of the hardware you use to access our services.

2. Information Usage

We employ the data we gather for a number of objectives, such as:

2.1 Offering Assistance

  • To handle money processing, ride request processing, and driver-rider matching.
  • To answer questions and offer customer service.

2.2 Enhancing Provisions

  • To examine usage trends and enhance our services' usability and functionality.
  • To carry out research and provide fresh features and offerings.

2.3 Security and Safety

  • To guarantee the security and safety of our users, drivers, and platform.
  • To authenticate users and stop fraud.

2.4 Promotion and Interaction

  • To send out news, updates, and exclusive deals.
  • To disseminate crucial information about our services, like notices of new policies and services.

3. Information Sharing

We might disclose your information to outside parties in the following situations:

3.1 With Service Providers

  • Third-party service providers that work on our behalf and handle tasks like payment processing, data analysis, and customer assistance may receive access to your information.

3.2 With Business Partners

  • In order to provide joint services or advertise special offers, we might disclose information to our business partners.

3.3 For Legal Purposes

  • We might divulge your data in accordance with the law or in response to a legitimate legal demand, like a subpoena or court order.

3.4 Upon Your Consent

  • If you provide us specific consent, we may disclose your information to outside parties.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

4.1 Access and Update

  • Through your account settings, you can access and update your personal data.

4.2 Opt-Out

  • You can contact us directly or follow the unsubscribe instructions in our emails to opt out of receiving promotional messages.

4.3 Data Erasure

  • By getting in touch with our customer service, you can ask that your account and personal data be erased.

5. Security

We put in place appropriate security measures to guard against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of your personal data. Nevertheless, we are unable to provide a 100% secure transmission via the internet or electronic storage solution.
