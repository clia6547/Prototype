Frequently Asked

What should I do if my driver hasn't arrived yet?

If your driver hasn't arrived within the expected time, please check the app for real-time updates on their location. If there appears to be a delay, you can contact the driver directly through the app or reach out to our customer support for assistance.

Can I cancel a ride, and is there a cancellation fee?

You can cancel a ride through our app. Cancellations made more than 5 minutes after booking may incur a fee to compensate the driver for their time. Please check our Cancellation Policy for detailed information.

How are the fares calculated?

Fares are calculated based on the distance of the trip, the type of service selected, and local demand at the time of your ride request. Our app provides a fare estimate before you confirm your booking.

How do I report a lost item?

If you've left something in the vehicle, you can report a lost item through our app under the 'Manage Trips' section, or contact our customer support directly. We will do our best to help you recover your item.

Is it possible to request a specific driver?

While we cannot guarantee that you will be matched with a specific driver, you can increase the likelihood by booking rides at similar times and locations as your preferred driver’s usual schedule. Our service is designed to provide the quickest and most efficient match based on your current location.

What safety measures are in place for riders?

Your safety is our top priority. All drivers are thoroughly vetted before they join our platform, which includes background checks and driving history reviews. Our app also features real-time GPS tracking, an emergency button, and the ability to share your ride details with trusted contacts.
